Information Letter

Investigating how People rate Utility

Dear participant,
Welcome to our study! This takes place at the Faculty of Social and Behavioral Sciences of the University of Amsterdam (UvA).

It is important that you learn about the procedure of this study before it starts, so please read the following text carefully. If anything is unclear to you, feel free to ask the researcher directly, or contact us through email: c.e.stevenson@uva.nl. We are happy to answer any questions you may have.
Goal of the study: This study examines how people rate utility/feasibility of responses in a task.
Study procedure: In this study you will rate the utility of 3 sets of 100 responses.
This study takes approximately 20 minutes.
Voluntary Participation: Your participation in this study is voluntary: you are not obligated to participate. You may decide to stop your participation during the study, and you can also decide to withdraw after the study. You do not have to provide a reason for stopping.
Privacy: We do not collect any personal data in this study.
Data sharing: The results of this study will be shared with other researchers and/or will be shared through a public database (open access). The results may be used in other future research, which may investigate a different topic than the study you are currently participating in.
Further information: If you have any questions about the study, either before you participate or afterwards, please feel free to contact the principal investigator: dr. Claire Stevenson, c.e.stevenson@uva.nl. You can direct any formal complaints about this study to the member of the Ethics Review Board of the Faculty of Social and Behavioral Sciences of the University of Amsterdam, Dr. Lourens Waldorp, l.j.waldorp@uva.nl.

Kind regards,
Dr. Claire Stevenson
c.e.stevenson@uva.nl
